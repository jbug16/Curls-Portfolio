Brief

This portfolio website, built by me, demonstrates my skills in web and video game development using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. What I Worked On:

Hero Section

The hero section is the eye-catching top part of a webpage, designed to grab visitors' attention right away. It typically features a large background image or video, a catchy headline, and a call-to-action button. I decided to include a photo of myself and a few buttons that take visitors to important parts of my site like my portfolio and resume.

How I Achieved This:

  • Set the background as a soft color to bring attention to the information.
  • Included a short about me under my name to provide a few details immediately on click.
  • Used Font Awesome icons to link my social media and contact information to visitors and potential employers.
