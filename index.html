About Me

I am a passionate Game Development student at Florida Polytechnic University, dedicated to creating engaging and innovative digital experiences. Originally from North Fort Myers, Florida, I moved to Lakeland to immerse myself fully in the world of game development and to hone my skills in both programming and design.

Throughout my journey, I have developed a strong foundation in game mechanics and design, utilizing tools like Unity, Unreal Engine, and GameMaker. My experience ranges from developing combat systems and dialogue mechanics using Unreal Engine's Blueprints and C++, to crafting UI design and game mechanics for mobile games in Unity. I have also been an integral part of teams for game jams and competitions, where I contributed to projects featuring physics-based mechanics, AI systems, and endless gameplay loops.

In addition to my game development skills, I have a solid background in web development. Proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, I've built responsive websites with features like automated email confirmations and Google Sheets integration. My freelance work on Fiverr includes creating simple 2D video games and visually appealing webpages, blending my knowledge of web development with my love for game design.

I approach each project with enthusiasm, constantly pushing myself to learn new technologies and explore creative boundaries. My goal is to bring unique, high-quality experiences to the world of games and interactive media, reflecting the passion that has driven me since childhood.
